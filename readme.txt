Display the Storystack widget below your posts.

== Description ==

Storystack is a content recommendation service. Our plugin displays a customized widget under each post, which shows recommended content from within your site. You can also choose to monetize your site, by allowing sponsored content from our advertisers.

== Installation ==

Signup for an account at storystack.com, and create a widget for your site. Grab the consumer key for your widget, and save it within WordPress on the Settings -> Storystack page.
